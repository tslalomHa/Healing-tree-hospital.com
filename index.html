About Healing Tree Hospital

Healing Tree Hospital is a private hospital located in the center of Mekelle, in front of the main Ethio Telecom office. It was established in 2010 by two siblings, Dr. Amit Hilemariam and Dr. Tsegay Hailemariam.

Our History & Growth

The shareholders are physicians with high reputation and rich experience. They have been practicing medicine for over 15 years in various specialties, including internal medicine, general & pediatric surgery, obstetrics, and gynecology.

The hospital started its service with less than 10 inpatient beds and one operation table in 2010. Since then, the hospital's service has grown significantly in both quantity and quality, adapting to the needs of our community.

Our Commitment

We are well-equipped with modern medical technology and staffed by a dedicated team committed to ensuring the best possible care for our patients. Our focus on patient well-being and clinical excellence has made Healing Tree Hospital one of the most preferred healthcare providers by patients and families in Mekelle and throughout Tigray.

The total bed capacity has now reached to more than hundred with four new operation theatre ,recovery rooms ,modern labrator,10 fully equipped intensive care units for adult with mechanical ventilators ,NICU and 6 emergency beds available in the emergency rooms
